NEWSBOX

Welcome


To use this website all you need to do is type a youtube URL on the search bar and click go!
Make sure the URL is a that of a video and not a Youtube playlist. After a brief introduction the video will momentarily start and omit any youtube commercials.

Once the video starts you will notice a red strip containing text which moves across the video you are watching. Don't worry, this is not part of the video link you entered, rather it is an added function this website provides. The moving white text are headlines of news articles with varying topics chosen at random between fifteen different news subjects. Each news topics will appear a total of three times before a new batch of news is queried. Each news query returns a batch of ten news articles.

If

while watching your favorite Youtube video you find a headline that interest you,
simply pause the moving text by hovering over it. Once paused you will have the option of either:
adding the news article or
remove it permanently.

If you choose to add the news article, that headline will be removed from the news strip and it will be stored inside the NewsBox located below the video you are watching.
if you choose to remove the news article, it will be removed from the news strip and other articles will populate in its stead.

Feel free to add several news articles while you enjoy watching your video. Once you decided you want to review the news articles you saved you can go below the video you watched and hover over the news article which will be placed inside a news card inside the aforementioned NewsBox. To view the full link, click on "read more" and a new tab will open with the full news article.

I hope you enjoy your visit. Feel free to contact me for any website inquiries.
